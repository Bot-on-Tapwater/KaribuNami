Karibu Nami aims to address the challenge of efficiently accessing and understanding information about local businesses and services when one visits an unfamiliar place

About Karibu Nami

Our app is designed to assist travelers, tourists, and anyone exploring new areas who seek quick and user-friendly access to information about local establishments. It is adaptable to various locales and regions

Current search methods provide fragmented data on places like hotels, barbershops, supermarkets, and schools near the user's location. The problem is that this information is often not well visualized and challenging for users to digest and utilize effectively.

Our application is proudly open-source. We invite you to explore our repository on GitHub and actively contribute to enhance the app's functionality and make it better. Click on the link below.

How to use the app

Step 1

Location access

Our app is designed to help you locate places that are near your location, for you to explore. To do this, the app will need to get your location. You can give access when you see the pop-up.

Step 2

Enter prompt

Enter the name of places you want to look up. Our application will furnish you with a comprehensive list of nearby establishments and points of interest relative to your current geographical position

Step 3

Browse the results

Peruse the search results as you try and find one that fits your preference. Detailed information, including ratings, reviews, and other pertinent details about each location, will be readily available for your evaluation.
